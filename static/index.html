About Question Generator

Welcome! This application uses advanced Google Gemini AI to instantly generate custom multiple-choice quizzes tailored to any academic subject or specific topic you want to study.

Built as an interactive practice dashboard, it scores your answers in real time, provides instant explanations, and lets you export clean worksheets for printing.
